## Executive Summary

The ADEO Product Grade (APG) Framework is a structured maturity model designed to assess and elevate the capabilities of product teams across ADEO's digital portfolio. By providing a common benchmark for product management practices, APG enables standardized assessment, harmonized methodologies, and strategic alignment to support ADEO's digital transformation initiatives like Global Ready, Tech Radar, and UX Bar.

APG facilitates:
- **Standardized Maturity Assessment**: Benchmark products against refined criteria for strategy, delivery, and customer focus
- **Methodological Harmonization**: Consistent approaches across product lifecycles for better cross-platform coordination
- **Talent Mobility**: Clear progression paths for PMs across products and platforms
- **Portfolio Management**: Data-driven insights for product optimization, extension, or sunset decisions

## Framework Overview

### Core Dimensions
APG evaluates product teams across five key dimensions:

1. **Customer Centricity** - Focus on user needs, feedback integration, and customer experience
2. **Strategy** - Vision alignment, roadmap planning, and market positioning
3. **Delivery** - Execution efficiency, quality assurance, and agile practices
4. **Data Usage** - Analytics-driven decisions, metrics tracking, and performance measurement
5. **Team Enablement** - Skills development, collaboration, and organizational support

### Maturity Levels
Teams are assessed on a 4-level scale, with clear progression criteria:

| Level | Description | Characteristics |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **1 - Emerging** | Basic awareness of product practices | Reactive approach, limited structure, ad-hoc processes |
| **2 - Developing** | Structured processes emerging | Defined roles, basic metrics, some customer focus |
| **3 - Established** | Mature, consistent execution | Data-driven decisions, cross-functional collaboration, proactive optimization |
| **4 - Leading** | Industry-leading excellence | Innovative practices, predictive analytics, continuous evolution |

### Scoring Methodology
- **Assessment Scale**: 1-4 points per dimension (1 = Emerging, 4 = Leading)
- **Overall APG Score**: Average across all dimensions
- **Evaluation Method**: Self-assessment + peer review + leadership validation
- **Frequency**: Annual baseline + quarterly progress reviews

## Detailed Dimension Criteria

### 1. Customer Centricity

| Level | Criteria | Evidence |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **1 - Emerging** | Basic customer awareness; feedback collected sporadically | User interviews conducted occasionally; no systematic feedback loops |
| **2 - Developing** | Regular customer research; user personas defined | Customer feedback integrated into some decisions; basic user journey mapping |
| **3 - Established** | Customer-centric culture; validated user needs drive priorities | Regular user testing, NPS tracking, customer success metrics monitored |
| **4 - Leading** | Predictive customer insights; proactive experience optimization | Advanced analytics for user behavior prediction; continuous UX optimization |

### 2. Strategy

| Level | Criteria | Evidence |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **1 - Emerging** | Tactical focus; limited strategic planning | Product roadmap exists but not regularly updated or aligned with business goals |
| **2 - Developing** | Basic strategic alignment; quarterly planning | Roadmap connected to OKRs; competitive analysis conducted periodically |
| **3 - Established** | Data-informed strategy; market-driven decisions | Regular market research, stakeholder alignment, risk assessment integrated |
| **4 - Leading** | Visionary strategy; ecosystem thinking | Long-term platform strategy, innovation pipeline, cross-platform synergies optimized |

### 3. Delivery

| Level | Criteria | Evidence |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **1 - Emerging** | Ad-hoc delivery; inconsistent quality | Basic agile practices; frequent delays and quality issues |
| **2 - Developing** | Structured delivery processes; basic quality controls | Sprint planning, code reviews, basic CI/CD implemented |
| **3 - Established** | Efficient delivery; high-quality outputs | Automated testing, continuous delivery, performance metrics tracked |
| **4 - Leading** | Optimized delivery; predictive quality | Advanced DevOps, A/B testing, automated performance optimization |

### 4. Data Usage

| Level | Criteria | Evidence |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **1 - Emerging** | Limited data awareness; anecdotal decisions | Basic usage metrics collected; decisions based on intuition |
| **2 - Developing** | Key metrics defined; basic analytics | Product metrics dashboard, AARRR framework applied |
| **3 - Established** | Data-driven decisions; comprehensive analytics | Advanced segmentation, cohort analysis, predictive modeling |
| **4 - Leading** | Predictive analytics; AI-augmented insights | Machine learning for user behavior, automated insights generation |

### 5. Team Enablement

| Level | Criteria | Evidence |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **1 - Emerging** | Basic team structure; limited skills development | Roles defined but training ad-hoc; siloed work |
| **2 - Developing** | Cross-functional collaboration; basic training | Regular team ceremonies, skills assessments conducted |
| **3 - Established** | High-performing teams; continuous learning | Knowledge sharing culture, mentorship programs, performance coaching |
| **4 - Leading** | Empowered, autonomous teams; innovation culture | Self-organizing teams, innovation time allocated, external best practices integrated |

## Assessment Process

### Phase 1: Framework Validation (Month 1)
- **Stakeholder Workshops**: Gather input from Product, Engineering, Design, and Performance leadership
- **Pilot Assessment**: Test framework with 2-3 product teams
- **Refinement**: Adjust criteria based on feedback and real-world applicability

### Phase 2: Baseline Assessment (Month 2)
- **Self-Assessment**: Teams complete APG questionnaire (digital tool)
- **Peer Review**: Cross-team validation for objectivity
- **Leadership Validation**: Product Leadership reviews and approves scores
- **Consolidated Report**: Organization-wide maturity heatmap and insights

### Phase 3: Action Planning (Month 3)
- **Gap Analysis**: Identify specific improvement areas per dimension
- **Action Plans**: 3-6 month roadmaps with measurable outcomes
- **Resource Allocation**: Training, tools, and coaching prioritized based on gaps

### Phase 4: Continuous Improvement (Ongoing)
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Progress tracking and score updates
- **Training Integration**: APG results inform capability development programs
- **Portfolio Optimization**: Maturity insights guide product investment decisions

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|------------|-----------|
| Resistance to assessment | Medium | Leadership sponsorship, transparent communication |
| Subjectivity in scoring | High | Peer review process, clear criteria definitions |
| Resource constraints | Medium | Phased rollout, prioritize high-impact teams first |
| Changing priorities | Low | Annual reassessment cadence, flexible action plans |
